# [06] SYSTEM_DIAGNOSTICS: PREFERENCES & CALIBRATION

## I. INTERFACE PROTOCOLS
- **SKIP_BOOT**: Allows bypassing the 3-act cinematic for rapid testing.
- **TUTORIAL_DATA**: One-click purge of onboarding flags.
- **SWITCHES**: High-contrast `SwitchButton` component.
  - **ACTIVE**: Cyan (#00ffff) glow | Label: `DISABLE [ACTIVE]`
  - **INACTIVE**: Orange (#ff6600) glow | Label: `ENABLE [NULL]`

## II. MAINFRAME CALIBRATION
- **CRT_SCANLINES**: Toggles the global visual post-processing overlay.
- **GLITCH_EFFECTS**: Toggles random character-scrambling and UI jitter events.

## III. AUDIO ENGINE (CURATED)
Consolidated into 5 high-fidelity synthesis tracks (126 BPM):
1.  **CORE_LOGIC**: Deep industrial with heavy sidechaining.
2.  **NEON_BREACH**: Aggressive cyberpunk with square-wave leads.
3.  **LIQUID_DATA**: Ambient tech with triangle-wave pads.
4.  **VOID_SIGNAL**: Minimal/Eerie with sine-wave sub-frequencies.
5.  **GRID_RUNNER**: Driving synthwave with sawtooth drive.

## IV. COMBAT PROTOCOLS
- **AUTO-PAUSE**: Freezes simulation at the end of each swarm for planning.
- **PERMANENT_RANGE_HUD**: Toggles global range visibility for all placed nodes.

## V. SYSTEM DIAGNOSTICS
- **LIFETIME_RECORDS**: Direct readout of Swarm Depth and Purge Count.
- **PURGE_STATS**: Red-level (#ff3300) button for total hardware reset.
