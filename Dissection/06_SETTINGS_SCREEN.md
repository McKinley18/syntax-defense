# [06] SETTINGS_SCREEN: SYSTEM_DIAGNOSTICS

## I. CONFIGURATION_MODULES
Three distinct grid-layout modules:
- **INTERFACE**: CRT Scanlines, Glitch Events, Auto-Pause, Range Display.
- **AUDIO**: SFX Mute, Ambient Mute, Volume Sliders (0-1.0).
- **DIAGNOSTICS**: Reset XP, Persistence Purge, Performance Stats.

## II. PERSISTENCE (LOCAL_STORAGE)
- `syntax_crt_enabled`: Toggle 3px horizontal scanlines.
- `syntax_glitch_enabled`: Background distortion effects.
- `syntax_auto_pause`: Pause at wave completion.
- `syntax_show_ranges`: Static tower range visibility.
- `syntax_skip_intro`: Bypass cinematic boot.
