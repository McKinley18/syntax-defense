# Tower & Combat Mechanics (Verified Production)

## Act 1: Intelligent Targeting Profiles
- **Pulse MG:** Furthest progress (Closest to Kernel).
- **Railgun:** Highest current HP (Tank Buster).
- **Frost Ray:** Fastest non-frozen unit.
- **Blast Nova:** Densest cluster (Splash value).
- **Tesla Link:** Shielded units first.

## Act 2: Proximity Synergies (Tactical Links)
- **Neural Link:** Adjacency (2.0 tiles) of SAME type adds raw damage.
- **Glitch Slow:** Frost Ray near Pulse MG grants MG slowing bullets.
- **Overclock Boost:** Tesla Link near any tower adds +30% fire rate.
- **Visuals:** High-fidelity cyan beams connect synergized towers.

## Act 3: Enemy Signatures
- **Glider:** 10HP (50HP in standard game), Triangle visual, High Speed.
- **Behemoth:** Thermal Shields (Cyan pulse) + Splitter (into 2 Striders).
- **Strider:** Circular visual + 20% Splitter chance (into 3 Gliders).
